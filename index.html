As a patient with emergency, I would like to have knowledge of the availability of the logistical support by hospitals so that at times of emergency, I can make brisk decisions.

Covihelp brings the logistical part of hospitals i.e beds, oxygen supply, ventilator in an area, in one platform, so that the time required to search for the amenities is saved. It also allows patients to book beds and oxygen cylinders.
